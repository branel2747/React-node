Rector

María Augusta Hermida Palacios

She is an Architect by the University of Cuenca, with a Master in Architectural Projects and Computerization of the Architectural Project. She holds a PhD in Architecture from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia. She is a senior lecturer at the Faculty of Architecture and Urbanism of the University of Cuenca and director of the Sustainable Cities Research Group - LlactaLAB.

Former Manager of Hidropaute, Business Unit of CELEC-EP; Head of the Environmental Management Unit of Hidropaute; Undersecretary of Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency of Ecuador and Executive Director of the Environmental Management Commission of the Municipality of Cuenca.
